About Me

M.S. Sungjae Park specialized in Spatial Data Science. His primary research interests lie in spatial statistics and spatial machine learning, with a particular focus on population movement and real estate prices. He predominantly uses tools such as ARCGIS and R, and frequently employs spatial regression analysis utilizing random effects and the random forest technique.
